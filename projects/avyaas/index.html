Avyaas Learning Ecosystem

Target Audience: 100,000+ Students | iOS, Android, and Web Solutions

Avyaas is a customized Learning Management System (LMS) and exam preparation suite engineered specifically to address the academic needs of students and chartered accountant aspirants in Nepal.

Architectural Achievements

  • Scaled microservices backend architecture to support over 100,000 registered users with zero downtime.
  • Consistently ranked #1 Trending in the local Play Store under the Education category during launch and exam seasons.
  • Average engagement time of 3 minutes per user in standard sessions and peak engagement of 12 minutes.
  • Implemented content management systems allowing teachers to securely upload high-definition video modules, quiz metrics, and subscription plans.
